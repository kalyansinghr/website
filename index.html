Purity Beyond Expectation.

Since 1991, Dvara waters® bottled water has been designed to be a perfect complement to everyday moments. At Dvara waters®, we always create a positive and optimistic approach to bottled water, rallying everyone to enjoy a reliably fresh beverage that enlivens the body and mind. Since our launch, we've been grounded in providing pure, clean water in a sustainable way, and we're proud to continue that tradition into the future. sustainability

HybridBottle

We use up to 50% plant-based renewable material and recycled PET plastic.

Aluminum cans and bottle

These are made from up to 70% recycled aluminum.

PureFill 2.0

We're adding more than 100 packageless water dispensers across the country.

How2Recycle Labels

These labels provide recycling information and help to give materials a second life.

Lighter-Weight bottle

Our new bottle design will reduce over 30% of plastic by weight.

World Without Waste

The company and its global network of bottling partners will tackle the ambitious goal through a renewed focus on the entire packaging lifecycle

Purification Process

Mineralisation

We add back the right amount of vital minerals (Magnesium & Potassium compounds) making our water healthy & giving it a sweet taste.

Re-Ozonisation

Helps to maintain purity and quality in sealed condition of the bottle.

Sand Filteration

Removes Unwanted Particles upto 30 microns.

Carbon Filteration

Gets rid of colour, odour and harmful pesticides. [double filtration]

Micron filtration

Ensures that even the smallest micron-particles (upto 0.45microns) are removed from the water.

Quality Control

Every bottle has a code printed stating the batch number, date, time and manufacturing location. Stringent quality control checks are done for each & every batch so that Bisleri’s quality standards are met.
